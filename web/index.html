📥 Import from Facebook Marketplace

Grab a whole page of Marketplace results at once. You only do step 1 once, ever.

  1. One-time setup: drag this button up onto your bookmarks bar → Grab FB listings No bookmarks bar showing? Press Ctrl+Shift+B.
  2. On the Facebook Marketplace Property Rentals page, set your filters and scroll down so listings load.
  3. Click the Grab FB listings bookmark. It copies what's on screen.
  4. Come back here, click in the box below and paste (Ctrl+V), then Import.
